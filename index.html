Lipedema Recovery

Specialized recovery support for lipedema surgery

With lipedema, recovery is a crucial part of the outcome. The way swelling, comfort, mobility and daily routines are managed after discharge can directly influence your progress, stability of results and overall experience. We support you with structured, surgeon-aligned care in a calm recovery apartment—so you know what’s normal, what to do next, and when to act.

Typical stay: 10–21 days
  • Higher-frequency professional care sessions
  • Support for compression routines and safe mobility progression
  • Fast escalation to your clinic for any deviations
How we help

We coordinate closely with your surgeon/clinic and help you stay consistent with the recovery plan: comfort support, daily-care routines (as indicated), progress check-ins, and clear guidance from discharge to a confident return to daily life.
