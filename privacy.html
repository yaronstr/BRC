Privacy & Medical Disclaimer

This page summarizes how we handle inquiries and clarifies service boundaries.

Non-medical service disclaimer

Barcelona Recovery Care provides non-medical recovery care and coordination aligned with the treating surgeon’s instructions. We do not diagnose, prescribe medications, or modify clinical instructions. All medical decisions remain the responsibility of the surgeon/clinic.

Data we collect
  • Contact details (name, phone/WhatsApp, email)
  • Basic inquiry information (procedure type, dates)
  • Messages you send us
How we use data
  • To respond to your request and provide a quote
  • To coordinate logistics and recovery sessions if booked
  • To communicate with your surgeon/clinic when needed (with your consent)
Retention & deletion

We keep your inquiry data only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with applicable obligations. You can request access or deletion by contacting us.
