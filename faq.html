Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for patients and clinics. If you have a specific question, contact us confidentially.

Contact us
Is this a medical service?

No. We provide non-medical recovery care and coordination aligned with the surgeon’s instructions. We do not diagnose, prescribe or modify treatment.

Do you include accommodation?

Yes. Your recovery program is delivered in private recovery apartments prepared for post-operative comfort.

What happens if something feels wrong?

We pause care, document the change and escalate immediately to your surgeon/clinic. Medical decisions always remain with them.

Do you support lipedema recoveries?

Yes. Lipedema recoveries are typically longer and recovery quality is a crucial part of success. We support you after discharge with structured, surgeon-aligned care: comfort and routine support, guidance on safe mobility progression, help staying consistent with compression routines (as indicated), and fast escalation to your clinic if anything deviates from the expected course.

How do I book?

Send your procedure details and dates via our confidential form or WhatsApp. We’ll recommend a program and send a quote.

Can a companion stay with me?

In many cases yes, depending on the apartment configuration and recovery needs. Tell us in advance.
